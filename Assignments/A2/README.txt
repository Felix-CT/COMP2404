Purpose: The emulation of a telephone company that keeps track of its subscribers,
and the calls that they send and receive.
Source Files: Array.cc, Call.cc, Control.cc, Date.cc, main.cc, Subscriber.cc, Telco.cc, Time.cc, View.cc
Header Files: Array.h, Call.h, Control.h, Date.h, Subscriber.h, Telco.h, Time.h, View.h

Instructions: To compile and launch the program, please extract all of the files
to a single folder, then navigate to the folder using the bash, and then enter
the following commands: "make", then enter "./a2". The program will then run.
